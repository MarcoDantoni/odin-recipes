Sushi


Description

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant. Plus, it’s easy and fun to make your own rolls at home, so you can put all your favorite ingredients into your perfect custom roll — here’s how!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Rice Vinegar

Steps

  1. Put the rice vinegar, sugar, salt, and kombu seaweed in a large, shallow pot and melt over low heat, stirring constantly, without bringing the liquid to a boil.
  2. The sushi-su will be ready when the sugar and salt have dissolved. At that point, you can also remove the kombu seaweed and transfer the sushi-su to a pitcher.
  3. How to cook sushi rice? Slowly and precisely. Rice should be boiled by using the correct ratio of rice to water. Generally, the ratio is 1:1, the parts of rice and water should equal each other in weight and volume.
